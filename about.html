Welcome to VEKS-Buddy Educational!

Who we are

CTU-Buddy is online platform that enhances the learning experience for students at City Technical University. This platform aims to provide a central hub for students to access a variety of features, facilitating effective schedule management, collaborative discussions, resource sharing, and direct communication with the institution. By offering a user-friendly and interactive interface, CTU-Buddy seeks to empower students in their academic journey, fostering a dynamic and enriched educational environment.

Our Mission

CTU-Buddy Team is online platform that enhances the learning experience for students at City Technical University with an unrelenting drive to deliver impactful learning solutions. We understand that your success is our success.

Our Vision

To be one of the worlds leading Learning Technology and E-Learning providers.

Our Values

  • Collaboration - We believe in partnership, teamwork, continuous learning, and empowerment where we have a win-win outcome.

  • Integrity and ethics - We believe in professionalism and open and long-term partnership.

  • Attitude - We engage through expeditious resolutions, innovation, and agility and eliminate waste in our approach.
